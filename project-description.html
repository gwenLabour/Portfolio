MY WORK

Dex's Diner - Waitress droid WA 7 3Ds Max - Substance - Unity HDRP

As part as my engineering school project, we were asked to create a 3d virtual scene representing a droid in an environment from the Star Wars universe. I chose Dex's dinner and the waitress droid because I like the intense and vivid colours and the seventies atmosphere. To enhance this atmosphere I particularly applied myself to the textures and lighting as well as the post-processing.
This scene is in real time using Unity HDRP pipeline and is suitable for real-time 3D applications and virtual reality.

S.A.M 3DsMax - Substance - Illustrator -
PremierePro - Unity C# - Kinect

As part of our Virtual Reality engineering courses, we developped an application with Kinect and created a novel cooperative game combining the use of our senses and agility. The main theme of this project was multi-sensory and the boundaries of sensorial realities. With my team we created a multiplayer game that encourages cooperation and where the gameplay focuses on challenging communication means due to the lack of senses of each player. The second phase of this one-week project allowed us to highlight the important issues to be corrected, in particular via post-experimental UX questionnaires designed specifically for this project (based on meCUE and AttrakDiff).

INEDIT 3Ds Max - Substance - Photoshop - Unity C#

INEDIT is a European project aimed at providing locally built bespoke furnitures. The project is still under development in order to have an application that can be used in virtual reality by a wide audience. In a team of four, we thought about the most judicious and straightforward way for users not familiar with new technologies. We made a prototype using a system of snapping the parts of the custom furniture.

ANTIDOTE 3Ds Max - Substance - Photoshop - PremierePro - Unity C#

We had the opportunity to work as a team on a project that could potentially be exhibited at Europe's largest virtual reality exhibition, Laval Virual (in 2019). The Antidote project is an escape game of solving enigmas and puzzles to break out from an abandoned hospital, recalling the Coronoa virus events of 2019.

Virtual Visit of a Biological Lab, Porsolt 3Ds Max - Substance - Unity C#

During the contest skills challenge MAYAM, a company was assigned to us and we had five days to put forward innovative ideas using new technologies. I had the opportunity to work with one of the world leaders in Preclinical Pharmacology, Porsolt. My team and I collaborated with the marketing branch to develop an application for customers to visit the laboratory online. The application also allows users to move around the laboratory and do some manipulations while interacting with the equipment. The UI guides the user through the tasks to be completed. My team won the first prize in this competition.

Virtual Reality, Slinging formation, Come In VR 3Ds Max - Substance - Illustrator - Unity C#

This project was realized during my internship in a company that creates virtual reality training in the field of industry, transport and logistics. The final application is a new virtual reality training solution for slinging. This innovative solution is designed for training centers or companies that wish to train their apprentices or employees in an impactful way. Thanks to this virtual reality immersion technology, the experience allows users to be more aware of safety during handling and lifting of loads. Indeed, today there are still too many work accidents and even deaths due to poor handling and risk minimization. The experience allows to learn how to evaluate the mass of a load, to locate its center of gravity then to choose the good lifting accessories according to its shape but also its weight as well as to choose the good slinging. I created the environment, the textures, did the integration, the lighting and the interfaces of this application.

Virtual Reality, Overhead crane formation, Come In VR 3Ds Max - Substance - Illustrator - Unity C#

This project was realized during my internship in a company that creates virtual reality training in the field of industry, transport and logistics. The final application is a new training solution in virtual reality for the practice of lifting loads with an overhead crane. This innovative solution is intended for training centers or companies that wish to train their apprentices or employees in an impactful way. This experience allows users to be more aware of safety during handling and lifting of loads. The experience allows to train to the safety rules when using overhead cranes and to discover the job of a bridge operator in a warehouse. The learner will be introduced to the different cranes, and learn the proper vocabulary of the field, as well as learn the handling and use of overhead cranes in complete safety. I created the environment, the textures, did the integration, the lighting and the interfaces of this application.
